# What Waits in the Woods
Adventure Game for Intro to Programming

## Game Overview
You are invited to a camping trip by some new firends, then disatser struck.

### World
The story is set up in the Davelburg Woods, 
where they will be able to explore some parts of the woods with the other characters, 
and find items such as a lighter and food for survival.

### Characters
There are 5 main characters
Jess: She is the classmate that invited you, you have English with her, super nice
Ryan: Swim Captain, likes to brag and very athletic
Cindy: Smart, all about the Earth, you have math together
Charles: Jess’s Boyfriend, You have Science with him
Penelope: Very quiet, somehow friends with all
And then there's a scene with mom, who encourages you to go to the trip

### Player Goals
To “win” you basically have to follow the group and find everyone, 
“losing” is not finding the last “missing” person.
